About The School

Learn more about our school, our values, and what makes us unique

S.R.V.N.S.S. V.H.S.S. (Sree Rama Vilasam Nair Service Society Vocational Higher Secondary School), established in 1957, is a prominent educational institution managed by the SRV NSS Karayogam No: 714 Group. Located in the serene rural area of Chirakkadavu in the Kottayam district of Kerala, the school operates under the Kanjirappally block. Our institution offers quality education for students from Grades 8 to 12, blending academic excellence with holistic development. With a rich legacy of dedication and commitment, the school has been a cornerstone in nurturing young minds.

Have Questions? We’ve Got Answers!

Here are some quick answers about our classes, activities, transport, and more. Feel free to reach out if you need extra info!

What makes your school classes special?

We have special classes at 9:00 AM designed specifically to enhance the quality and performance of our students.

Do you offer any extra coaching or remedial classes?

Yes, we offer additional support classes for students who need extra help in specific subjects. These are conducted after regular school hours or during designated time slots.

What subjects are covered in the special 9:00 AM classes?

These classes cover a range of subjects including Math, Science, and Language, with a focus on concept clarity, practice, and skill-building activities. We also take classes for subjects where portions haven't been fully covered on time. During exam periods, we use these sessions for thorough revisions to ensure better performance and understanding.

What is the exact location of the school?

The school is situated at Thekkethukavala, near Chirakkadavu Sree Mahadeva Temple, in Chirakkadavu Grama Panchayat. For the exact location, please refer to the location map provided below.

Do you offer school bus service?

Yes, we provide school bus service at an affordable price to ensure safe and convenient transportation for our students.

Does your school offer any special activities like Little Kites or Red Cross?

Yes, our school actively supports student development through various special activities such as NCC, Little Kites, Red Cross, and Scout & Guide programs. These initiatives help students build leadership skills, teamwork, and social responsibility.
